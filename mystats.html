Mutton Biryani

This delectable dish is a symphony of tastes and textures, with tender pieces of marinated mutton cooked to perfection, layered between fluffy rice infused with saffron, caramelized onions, and a medley of spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. The result is a tantalizing blend of savory and subtly spicy flavors, brought to life by the slow-cooking process that allows the ingredients to meld together harmoniously.
