Goa, located on the southwestern coast of India, is a coastal paradise renowned for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and rich history. This tiny state, often referred to as the "Pearl of the Orient," attracts travelers from around the world with its golden sands, palm-fringed shores, and clear blue waters.
